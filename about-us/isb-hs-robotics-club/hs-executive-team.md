# HS Executive Team

## Roles in the HS Executive Team

As of 2024, the HS Executive Team consists of 8 _de jure_ positions with some left vacant:

* Club President
* Vice President
* Club Secretary
* Lead Designer
* Lead Programmer
* Lead Engineer
* MS Robotics Coordinator
* Public Relations/Communications Exec.&#x20;
